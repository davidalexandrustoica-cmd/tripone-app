Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is TripOne free to use?
Yes, TripOne is completely free. Destination guides, live weather, comparisons and browsing are all free to use, no account needed.
How many destinations are covered?
Currently 20 top destinations worldwide: Santorini, Bali, Barcelona, Dubai, Paris, Rome, Lisbon, Tokyo, Amsterdam, Prague, Mykonos, Ibiza, New York, Bangkok, Phuket, Vienna, Budapest, Tenerife, Marrakech and Maldives. More are being added regularly.
Is the weather data real?
Yes. Weather data is live and updated in real-time using the OpenWeatherMap API. You see the actual current temperature and 7-day forecast for each destination.
Can I book flights and hotels through TripOne?
TripOne shows you the best options and prices, then links you directly to the booking platform (Skyscanner, Booking.com etc.) where you complete the reservation. We don't handle payments directly.
Is there an AI itinerary planner?
Not yet — it's in active development. Soon you'll be able to enter your destination, trip length and travel style and get a complete day-by-day plan generated for you. Want early access? Reach out — link in the founder section below.
Who is TripOne for?
TripOne is for anyone who wants to plan a trip without the hassle. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, a group of friends or a family — if you've ever had 10 tabs open trying to plan a vacation, TripOne is for you.

Who's behind TripOne

Built solo, from scratch

DS
David Stoica
Founder of TripOne · Student, West University of Timișoara

I'm a first-year International and European Law student in Timișoara, Romania, with a background in football analysis and a deep interest in how things actually get built — not just talked about.

TripOne started from a simple frustration: every time I planned a trip, I had 10 browser tabs open — one for flights, one for weather, one for hotels, one for "is this place safe." I wanted one place that just answered all of it. So I built it, from zero, without funding, without a technical background — learning as I went.

TripOne is still early, but it's real, it's live, and it's growing one destination at a time.
