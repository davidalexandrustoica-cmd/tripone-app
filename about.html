Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is TripOne free to use?
Yes, TripOne is completely free. Destination guides, weather, comparisons and browsing are all free. The AI Finder and Itinerary Generator use AI credits but are accessible to all users.
How many destinations are covered?
Currently 20 top destinations worldwide: Santorini, Bali, Barcelona, Dubai, Paris, Rome, Lisbon, Tokyo, Amsterdam, Prague, Mykonos, Ibiza, New York, Bangkok, Phuket, Vienna, Budapest, Tenerife, Marrakech and Maldives. More are being added regularly.
Is the weather data real?
Yes. Weather data is live and updated in real-time using the OpenWeatherMap API. You see the actual current temperature and 7-day forecast for each destination.
Can I book flights and hotels through TripOne?
TripOne shows you the best options and prices, then links you directly to the booking platform (Skyscanner, Booking.com etc.) where you complete the reservation. We don't handle payments directly.
How does the AI Itinerary work?
You tell us your destination, how many days, your travel style and daily budget. Our AI (powered by Claude) generates a complete day-by-day plan with morning, afternoon and evening activities, restaurant recommendations, estimated costs and insider tips — all personalized to your preferences.
What is the AI Destination Finder?
The Finder helps you choose where to go when you're not sure. You enter your budget, when you're traveling, who you're going with, what vibe you want, and what you want to avoid — and our AI recommends the top 3 destinations that match, with a personalized explanation for each.
Who is TripOne for?
TripOne is for anyone who wants to plan a trip without the hassle. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, a group of friends or a family — if you've ever had 10 tabs open trying to plan a vacation, TripOne is for you.
