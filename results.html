Safety & Warnings

Tourist pricingRestaurants near the caldera charge 2–3x more than local spots. Walk 5 minutes inland for real prices.
Cruise ship crowdsOia gets extremely crowded between 4–7pm when cruise passengers arrive. Visit the sunset spot early to get a good spot.
Donkey ridesAvoid riding donkeys up the steps from the port — it's considered animal abuse. Take the cable car (€6) instead.
PickpocketsGenerally safe, but watch your belongings in the busy market areas of Fira.

Discounts & Deals

Ferry combo dealsBook ferry + volcano tour together — typically saves €8–12 vs buying separately.
Shoulder season pricingVisit in May or October for 30–40% lower hotel prices with almost identical weather.
Book early for flightsPrices from Bucharest to Santorini start doubling after April. Lock in prices 6–8 weeks out.
Free museumsArchaeological Museum of Thira is free on specific national holidays. Check local calendars.
